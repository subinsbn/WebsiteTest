Welcome to Our Family

The water dispenser business segment carries the largest stock of Hot & Cold Water dispensers for both SURE & Dolphin brands, for use in office or home. Range of water coolers consists of different colors, sizes and functionality. About Our Company

SURE is Middle East’s biggest brand name suppliers of Climatcool air conditioners, SURE & Dolphin water dispensers, SURE water purification systems and leading turnkey solution providers for the PET beverage and packaging industry. Sure International LLC was established in 1998 to supply air-conditioning solutions (Climatcool Air-conditioners) and 5 gallon water dispensers (SURE + Dolphin) for the U.A.E
local market. The Climatcool Air-conditioners, is an industry leader known for its superior product performance and reliability. Range of products include Window Air-conditioners from 1 to 2.5 tons and Decorative Split from 1 to 15 tons comprised of wall, Cassette, Floor Ceiling and Free Standing types. Climatcool also has a full range of Packaged chiller systems from 1 to 400 tons with matching chilled water fan coil units and chilled water Double skin Air Handling units.
