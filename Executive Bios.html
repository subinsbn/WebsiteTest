Mr. S.B. Jain

Managing Director

S.B. Jain is the founder and chief mentor along with his son Jinesh Jain of the Sure Group. A post graduate from IIT Chicago, in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering, Mr. Jain is currently the guiding force behind new verticals and businesses. In his 32 years of experience in the Gulf, Mr. Jain has played an integral role in making Sure Technologies today as one of Middle East’s leading environmental focused company, providing the best air-conditioning, water and beverage & packaging technology solutions.

Group Building

Mr. Jain has executed dynamic ventures in Middle East and North Africa region in the field of air-conditioning. Through guidance and leadership, Mr. Jain along with his son, entered in the water dispenser and beverage & packaging solutions businesses. Sure is ow a leading brand in the water dispensers segment with a leading market share across the UAE and a strong brand recognition in the GCC markets. In the past decade, through strong relationship with TECH-LONG of China, SURE Technologies has executed number of bottling lines in the Middle East and North Africa regions, and today we have expanded the expertise to India and Singapore.

Global Vision

Growing in step with the global demand and opportunities, Sure along with our partners including Climatcool, TECH-LONG, UNIQUE and ROPV, we have expanded and grown in size to cater to the demands of our Clients. We have built a strong team of engineers and sales force to manage and handle installations, provide the best advice in solutions and after sales support. Our services and after sales support is one of our strengths and emphasis, we will work together to service and support any of our clients demand, and try and solve the problem in the shortest possible time. Our Mission is “Customer Satisfaction First, and to deliver world class products”.

Mr. Jinesh Jain

Executive Director

As Executive Director of Sure Group, Jinesh Jain has led pioneering relationships with leading beverage packaging manufacturers across the world, and today has spear-headed Sure Technologies as exclusive agents for TECH-LONG Machines, one of the largest and leading beverage packaging manufacturer in the World. Through guidance and leadership, Jinesh has commissioned and completed over 200 bottling lines to prestigious bottled water Companies across the Middle East and North Africa regions.

Prior to establishing the beverage and packing solutions business, Jinesh’s involvement in the Group’s core business activities in air-conditioning and water dispensers, has led the company to grow in innovation, technology and market share. He was instrumental in bringing energy-saving and high-tech water dispenser models, the first of its kind, to the Middle East. In addition, Jinesh’s business acumen was instrumental in developing strategic partnerships with leading power retailers like Carrefour, GIANT, eMax, Hyperpanda, etc. and distributors alike, and today, the water dispenser business has an 80% market share across the UAE area, with a strong brand recognition in the GCC markets.

Jinesh Jain graduated with Bachelors of Science from Drexel University, Philadelphia, USA; and MBA from American University of Dubai, UAE. Picture

Mr. Parin Sanghvi

Marketing Manager

As the Marketing Manager and Vertical Head of Sure Water Technologies, Parin Sanghvi is instrumental in the development of the Water Filtration business for the Group.

Parin Sanghvi is the driving force in building Sure Group water products portfolio, with a vision to be a leading water purification solutions provider globally. His strategy for growth has led Sure to build extensive relationships with leading water technology companies, such as Dow chemicals, Woongjing Chemicals, Kemflo, Harbin ROPV Development Center, etc.

Through his initiation from domestic purification systems and further diversification into Industrial purification systems and components, Sure Technologies has enjoyed consistent growth in scale and profitability.

Parin Sanghvi graduated with a Bachelors in Commerce from the Gujarat University and an MBA from Southern New Hampshire University.
